Case Studies

Project Overview

Industry: Healthcare Technology

Role: Scrum Master

Developed a mobile app enabling patients to book and manage medical appointments.

Challenges

  • Unclear requirements from multiple stakeholders.
  • Ensuring HIPAA compliance and data security.
  • Dependency on third-party APIs for hospital integration.
  • Slow feedback loops causing misalignment.

Approach

  • Implemented Agile best practices and improved backlog refinement.
  • Facilitated stakeholder collaboration through Sprint Reviews.
  • Introduced CI/CD and automated testing, reducing regression by 40%.

Outcome & Impact

  • Successfully launched the app within 6 months.
  • Reduced booking errors by 50% and increased efficiency by 40%.
  • Ensured HIPAA compliance while accelerating development.
